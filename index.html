Zichen "Charles" Zhang

Hi! I am Charles (张子晨)! I am a third-year student majoring mathematics and computer science at Macalester College advised by Prof. Andrew Beveridge and Susan Fox. I am also doing a research intern at Allen Institute for AI (AI2) advised by Luca Weihs.

My primary research interest lies in designing, developing, optimizing, and deploying safe vision-based deep reinforcement learning algorithms as well as their underlying mathematical principles to build autonomous (embodied, multi) agents that can perform in dynamic environments, solving real-world puzzles for social good. Recently, I've been working on adversarial reset-free reinforcement learning with sink states.
